Welcome to the American Legion Riders Chapter 365 page

On this site you will find information about the American Legion Riders organization.

We are a great organization that has a lot of fun, while helping our community raise funds to support our veterans, active duty military, and country!

Meetings are held the 4th Tuesday of the month at our host Post 365 at 7:00 pm in the Oak Room.
If you would like to join our organization, go to the membership page below, download an application and drop it off at the Post.

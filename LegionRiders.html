Welcome to American Legion Riders Chapter 365

On this page you will find information about the American Legion Riders organization.

We are a great group that has a lot of fun while helping our community raise funds to support our veterans, active duty military, and country!

Meetings are held the 4th Tuesday of the month at our host Post 365 in the Oak Room at 7:00 pm.

If you would like to join our organization, download an application from the membership page below and drop it off at the Post.

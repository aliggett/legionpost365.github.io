Post 365 Fish Fry Page

Welcome to the Legion Post 365 "Best in the Midwest" Fish Fry page.

Fish fry's are every Friday from 3:00 pm to 7:45

Call in, curbside, and the Dinning hall is open.
 **** FISH FRY CALL IN AND PICKUP INSTRUCTIONS ****

Call in - 618.345.2508.
Give operator your Name, Car model and color.
Give operator your order.
Operator will confirm order, give you a pickup time, cost, and an order number.
Please try and remember your order number.
Come to the Post at assigned time.
Pull into the lot give Robert your order number and pull to assigned lane for pickup.
Please have correct change or check for our carhops.
Please remember, we are all volunteers and are doing the best we can under the circumstances.
Please be kind and tip your carhop, she is working very hard to give you the best service we can.

On behalf of Post 365, we greatly appreciate all the support from the area.

We use the best Icelandic Cod, flash frozen on the boat and shipped to us.
Our breading is formulated at the Post using only the best ingredients
and the original recipe from 1956.
Our catfish is farm raised in Missouri and delivered to us fresh.

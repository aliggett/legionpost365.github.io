"Best in the Midwest" Friday Fish Fry

Every Friday from 3:00 pm to 7:45 pm

Call-in, curbside pickup, and dine-in available in the hall.

Call-In & Pickup Instructions

  • Call to order: 618.345.2508
  • If there's a hold, please be patient — we have 4 people on the phones.
  • Give your name, car model, and car color.
  • Give your order — the operator will confirm, give pickup time, cost, and order number.
  • Please remember your order number.
  • Arrive at assigned time and pull into the lot.
  • Give Robert your order number and pull to assigned lane for pickup.
  • Please have correct change or check for our carhops.
  • We are all volunteers doing our best — please be kind and tip your carhop!

On behalf of Post 365, thank you for all the support from the area.

We use the best Icelandic Cod (flash frozen on the boat and shipped to us).
Our breading is formulated at the Post using only the best ingredients and the original recipe from 1956.
Our catfish is farm-raised in Missouri and delivered fresh.
